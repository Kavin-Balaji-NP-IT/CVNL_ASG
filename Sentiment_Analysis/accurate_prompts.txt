ACCURATE PROMPT STATEMENTS FOR SENTIMENT ANALYSIS MODEL
======================================================

Based on model testing, these prompts give the most accurate results:

STRONG POSITIVE (99%+ accuracy):
- "Amazing service, absolutely love it!"
- "Great food and beautiful airport"
- "Perfect flight, wonderful staff"
- "Love the friendly and helpful crew"
- "Excellent experience, highly recommend"

CLEAR NEGATIVE (99%+ accuracy):
- "Worst service I have ever experienced"
- "Terrible flight, absolutely horrible"
- "Hate this airline, never flying again"
- "Disgusting food and rude staff"
- "Awful experience, completely disappointed"
- "Very bad service, totally unacceptable"

SIMPLE NEUTRAL (80%+ accuracy):
- "Standard check-in process"
- "Regular airport facilities"
- "Standard boarding procedure"
- "Normal flight experience" (may vary)
- "Typical airline service" (may vary)

MODEL STRENGTHS:
- Excellent at detecting strong emotional language
- Works best with clear positive/negative words
- Struggles with subtle or mixed sentiments
- Most accurate with simple, direct statements

AVOID THESE (Lower accuracy):
- Mixed sentiment: "Good food but bad service"
- Subtle emotions: "The service was okay"
- Complex statements: "Outstanding customer service today"
- Ambiguous phrases: "Average experience, nothing special"
